Text Morph Animation Test

Text Morph Animation Test is a After Effects experiment where I explored the use of keyframe graphs to create smooth, fluid animations. The piece begins with 3D blocks flying through space before morphing into a humorous text that reads "I eat dirt." I used keyframe animation to ensure smooth transitions, giving the piece a polished and dynamic feel.
